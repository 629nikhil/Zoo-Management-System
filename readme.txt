A bullet list of any assumptions that were made.

1. Admin have to add the discount... Not any discount already exist.
2. To speacial deals are active always.
3. Animals having the same type have same voice.
4. Any one can explore the zoo, whether they have zoo membership or not.
5. while buying the tickets, if the user buy tickets in the multiple of two then he get 15% discount and if they buy in the multiple of 3 then they get 30 percent discount.
6. People having age less than 18 or greater than 60, they get the discount automatically.
	less than 18 get 10%.
	greater than 60 get 20%
7. For visit Animals or attraction option , visitor need to compulsory have any Membership .
8. Input should be given in correct form i.e - when asking for a price or any numerical value only numbers should be given as a input. Although i have handled some of these , but some are still left , as handling these were not a priority for this assignment.
9. total number of visiters include ticketetvisitor and non ticketed also.
10. All money is in rupees.
